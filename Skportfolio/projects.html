Indian Census report 2011

The 2011 Census of India was the 15th national census and the 7th after independence, conducted in two phases:
1.Listing & Housing Census: April–September 2010
2.Population Enumeration: February 9–28, 2011
It was the first census to include biometric data for the National Population Register, paving the way for Aadhaar IDs.
